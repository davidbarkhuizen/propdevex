Why we started ?

Fisher Roeland Properties was founded in 2013 as a joint venture between Jennifer Rose and Lisa Roeland after identifying a need in the market to fuse a passion for property development with a passion for people.

Base of operations

Based in Linbro Park, Sandton and operating throughout most of South Africa’s major cities, Fisher Roeland Properties’ innovative approach and dedication to service excellence almost instantly established the company as experts and the preferred partner for developments in highly sought-after locations.
